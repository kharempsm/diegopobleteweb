About Me

During my professional career, I have led initiatives aimed at boosting visibility, growth, and profitability through digital marketing strategies and conversion rate optimization on digital platforms. I have actively collaborated in the development and implementation of comprehensive paid media strategies, utilizing platforms such as Google Ads, Meta, Linkedin, and TikTok to generate qualified traffic and increase the visibility of services or products. My focus lies in continuous campaign performance analysis, using tools like Google Analytics and attribution platforms to consistently optimize budgets and improve results.


Furthermore, as a CRO and metrics expert, I have developed strategies based on identifying opportunities for improvement in user experience, utilizing research, behavioral data analysis, and A/B testing to optimize key conversion components and micro-conversions. All of this work has been done in close collaboration with multidisciplinary teams (web development, UX/UI, agile teams, creatives, sales, among others), allowing me to gain a comprehensive view and apply efficient solutions.


Research has been a fundamental pillar in my career. I have applied a variety of methods to thoroughly understand users and the operating market, including benchmarking, competitive analysis, usability testing, AB testing, as well as dropout surveys or NPS. These research methods have provided valuable insights for the development and refinement of digital strategies.


I am convinced that the combination of solid data, creativity, and market experience are essential for success in digital marketing. My ability to quickly assimilate new knowledge has allowed me to effectively adapt to various contexts and challenges during my professional development.


As a Performance Leader and CRO, I am constantly updated on the latest trends and best practices in the field of digital marketing and conversion rate optimization by attending conferences, participating in expert communities, and seeking professional development and learning opportunities.
